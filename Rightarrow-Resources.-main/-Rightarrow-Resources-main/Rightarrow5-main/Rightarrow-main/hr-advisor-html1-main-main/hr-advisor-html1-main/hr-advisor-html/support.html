    Our Support Policy

    1. If the candidate that we have hired, leaves your organization before confirmation, we will hire another candidate free of cost
    2. Compliance Assistance: Our policy includes keeping clients updated on relevant labor laws and regulations, helping them maintain compliance.
    3. We offer assistance with HR software and tools, ensuring clients can effectively utilize these resources
